ABOUT US

    Greendome is an online Learning platform available for learners who want to improve themselves in order to stand out in their fields or career. This platform makes it easy. It has further presented the total experiences that learners need in terms of content, performance objectives and activities. It has also covered Curriculum of various courses as required by various local and international educational regulatory bodies. It includes well-structured lessons which are filled with a wide variety of fun and learning activities to build and challenge the competence of Learners in the use of those courses and to make serious learning truly exciting. The format of this online platform is clearly distinct. Each course begins with clearly stated learning objectives followed by required teaching and Learning activities. There are well graded evaluation questions to evaluate Learning. A way for students to receive and review feedback for their work. This includes grading and comments from their instructors. This is aimed at helping students to study independently, understand, and develop their cognitive, affective and psychomotor domain. This platform is made to be user Friendly, following an active teaching and Learning. It helps students develop a concrete understanding of various courses with carefully planned progression from one module to another, to meet learner’s needs and making them understand the required courses curriculum for a particular level of Learning. In addition, this platform supports options like interactive content including Lean forward video and audio features, such as Choose your own adventure and Learning path. This is about meeting student’s needs; providing them with an experience that doesn't just mirror a traditional classroom experience but help them to learn independently of time and place. Participants can also access the Course / Education / subject content over and over again. Greendome is also beneficial to the following group of persons.


    1. Career Starters, or those looking to re-enter the workplace after break 2. Career boulders, or those hoping to build upon existing skills.
    3. Career Switchers, or those looking for ways to expand opportunities.
    4. Career advancers or those hoping to advance their current fields.
    5. Career Launchers or those looking at starting a new career.
    6. Organizations who want to train their employees by organizing online training for team members and provide them the information quickly and practically.
